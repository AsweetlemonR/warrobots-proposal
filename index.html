General / Module Rework

T1 & T2 Module Pathway Rework Rework ▼
Proposal: Tier 1 and Tier 2 robots and titans should share the same module pathway as Tier 3 and Tier 4 units. The current disparity creates a significant barrier to entry and devalues lower-tier units in competitive and mid-game scenarios.

Request: Re-evaluate module slot access so that T1/T2 units receive equivalent module upgrade paths, ensuring progression consistency across all tiers.
